Lessons & Programs

From first splash to advanced strokes—there’s a class for every child.

Lesson Levels

Baby & Me (6–18 mo)

Parent-participation class focusing on water familiarity, comfort, and fun songs.

  • Max 8 families
  • 30 mins / class
  • Water acclimation
Beginner (3–6 yrs)

Kick, float, bubbles—build confidence with safety-first fundamentals.

  • Max 6 students
  • 30–40 mins
  • Front/back float
Intermediate (6–9 yrs)

Stroke foundations: freestyle, backstroke, breathing, and endurance.

  • Max 6 students
  • 40 mins
  • Side breathing
Advanced (9–12 yrs)

Refine strokes, starts & turns; prepare for meets and club tryouts.

  • Max 8 students
  • 45–60 mins
  • Starts & turns
